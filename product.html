Smart

  • Cleaning Area: The Envirobot has a designated cleaning area. This area can range from four to 10 square blocks. Envirobot is updated with real time information obtained from people that live and or work in the Envirobots cleaning area.
  • Charging Stations: Conveinently located in the center of the Envirobots cleaning area. The stations are solar powered and act as a home for the Envirobot. During adverse weather, high traffic times, or a myriad of other situations the Envirobot returns to the charging station.
  • Navigation: Using a wide array of sensors and mapping information the Envirobot navigates its way through the city. Constantly sensing the environment the Envirobot is able to easily navigate through the city.
  • Cleaning Scope: The scope of the cleaning can be predetermined by user defined parameters. Including scraping gum, picking up trash, cleaning poop and washing the surrounding area. Choose what you want your Envirobot to focus on and it will do the rest!

Simple

  • Convenient:Designed with conveinence in mind. The Envirobot is easily maintained, cared for, and used. Receiving constant updates from people in the surrounding area, the Envirobot only works when work needs doing, stayins safely tucked away the remainder of the time.
  • Job Status: Conveintly located within the Envirobot app. You can watch the status of a job from start to finish. After cleaning the area that the user pointed out the Envirobot will automatically update the job status. You can sign up to receive a text letting you know the job has been completed as well.
  • Maintenance The Envirobot is concious! Not really, but it is aware of when it needs updates. Everytime the Envirobot docks at its charging station it uploads its status to the server while also updating its software. Technicians follow the Envirobots updates and can remotely perform any necessary maintenance.

Green

  • Sustainably Produced: After the founders created a sustainably produced material, they were in the unique position to sustainably produce the Envirobot. As well as using sustainable techniques in the factories.
  • Solar Powered: Every part of the Envirobot company utilizes solar energy. From product creation to product delivery solar power used. Including factories, delivery trucks, the Envirobot, etc.
  • Keeping the Envrionment clean: Every day that the Envirobot is on the job, it prevents 10lbs of garbage from reaching the ocean. Protecting every environment along the way.
  • Gives Back: The founders believe in giving back to the community. In support of this value and mission they have pledged to give 20% of procedes to causes ranging from Enviromental to Human Rights.
